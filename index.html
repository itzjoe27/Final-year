About Study Assist

Study Assist was developed as part of a Software Engineering project at Portsmouth University. The application aims to help university students who struggle with self-discipline and need a supportive tool to manage their study sessions effectively.

Our goal is to create a comprehensive digital study tool that enhances student productivity and learning experience through intelligent study management and distraction control.
